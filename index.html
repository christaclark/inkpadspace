About Inkpad

Inkpad is a shared workspace with dedicated work stations. We bring solopreneurs together who share the desire to work in a community setting rather than an isolated home office. We've built an inspiring, open workplace for your creativity and business to thrive.

Inkpad offers dedicated desk space, great bandwidth, and pretty fair coffee in a well-designed collaborative work environment, right in the heart of Old Town Fort Collins.
